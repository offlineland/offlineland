How to use your steam account on browser

Open the Dev Tools on Steam by pressing Ctrl + F12.

Go to Storage tab -> Cookies, and copy the values for act and s.

On your browser, go to Manyland, open the devtools (F12), go to Storage -> Cookies. This time, paste the values from Steam, replacing what's already there.

Note: do not share these cookies with anyone! If you do, they'll be able to do exactly as above and log into your account.
