How to use your steam account on browser

Open the Dev Tools on Steam by pressing Ctrl + F12.

Click on the (1) Resource tab, then (2) Cookies, and copy the value of the s cookie (double-click on the value, then ctrl+c or drag it to notepad.)

The s cookie starts with s%3A.

On your browser, go to Manyland.com, then open the devtools again (F12).

Click on the (1) Storage tab, then (2) Cookies. This time, replace the value of the s with the one you copied from Steam.

Refresh. If everything goes well, you'll be logged-in on your steam account! You can now continue with the exporter.

If it didn't work, try going over the steps again.

Note: do not share this cookie with anyone! If you do, they'll be able to do exactly as above and log into your account.
